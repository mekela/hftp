Get involved

Our Chapter is looking for volunteer leaders!

You can get involved with your chapter in a very small way, and make a big contribution. Could you spare an hour a month? Would you like to help with a committee? Your board is looking for members to help with Social Events, the Newsletter, Welcoming new members, coordinating meeting sites, and more.

GET INVOLVED - WITH HFTP GLOBAL
Are you looking to get involved in your membership on a deeper level?

get involved

HFTP’s member volunteers are what make the association a strong, collaborative community.

It is your chance to help shape the association and hospitality industry whether on the local chapter level or globally.

Here are just some of the types of opportunities:

  • Participate on an advisory council or committee
  • Give an educational presentation
  • Help guide the direction of the Global association
  • Mentor young professionals to help them get started in the industry
  • Chapter Leadership Opportunites
  • Help oversee the direction of your chapter
  • Work with other professionals in your area
  • Assist with coordinating education and event planning
  • Help recruit new members at the chapter level
  • Click here to learn more about ways to get involved in HFTP.

Do you have a specific interest within your association? Volunteer to serve on an HFTP Global Committee or Council that fits your needs.
